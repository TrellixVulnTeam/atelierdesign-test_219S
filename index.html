A payment flow tailored to your client

With PAY-NXT we offer you the opportunity to optimize your transactional communication via an easy to integrate system. Safe, simple and fast. Everywhere and real-time, regardless of the payment method. PAY-NXT helps to improve the imperfections within the payment process of your customer.

Convenience & choice

Today companies are using multiple channels for transactional customer communication. This can be a website, a custom app, e-mail, chat or any other digital channel. At PAY-NXT we know this is the reality and we will assist you in the preparation of this multichannel payment process. A payment can be initiated and tracked in multiple channels at the same time. The status of your customer is synchronized everywhere in real-time. PAY-NXT will support you to integrate the best payment method in every channel so that your customer can easily, quickly and safely pay. Dynamic

Our emails and Pay Now buttons change color when they are paid or expired, and thus from request to receipt. But they can also show a current price, or reveal a QR code after (fast) payment. The possibilities are endless.

Reporting

PAY-NXT knows whether payment requests were received, opened, clicked and paid. You can see and leverage this valuable behavioral data in our dashboard, use it with PowerBI, or ingest via API or SFTP for usage in your own systems.

Real-Time

Track the status of every payment request in real-time and know instantly when someone paid - even if the money still takes a day or two to make it to your account. Depending on the payment method, you can act straight away, for last-minute or last-chance service.
